Bloomble

Welcome to Bloomble, a STEM-based word guessing game. You have 6 attempts to guess the correct word of the day. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Green indicates the letter is correct and in the right position, yellow indicates a correct letter in the wrong position, and grey indicates the letter is not in the word at all.

Strengthen your STEM vocabulary one word at a time. Good luck!
